Coconut Jones

Coconut jones was established September 30, 2018. She was inducted into the Zamorano family on Christmas morning in 2018. She is currently 4 years old.

Now you might ask... Coconut jones, what a unique name. And to that, I respond, "you are correct." Coconut jones' name was not always Coconut jones. She was first dubbed Coco. A few hours after being dubbed, I (her all knowing mother) did not like coco because it was too basic. I decided to call her Coconut instead. I, then, did not like Coconut alone, so I added a "jones" on the end and that is how Coconut jones was officially dubbed.
